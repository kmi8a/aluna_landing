Behind ALUNA

ALUNA is a project by Outline Creative Lab, led by artists Santiago Caro and Tixana Ospina alongside a multidisciplinary team of designers, engineers, producers, and makers from Colombia and the United States.

Lead Artist · Creative Direction

Founder of Outline Creative Lab. Industrial designer and audiovisual artist working across interactive art, broadcast, and bio-architecture in Colombia and the US.

Lead Artist · Brand & Communications
